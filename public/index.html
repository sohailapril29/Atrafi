Technical Directors & Team

Technical Director

NAIF AL JOUFI
Managing Director & Co-founder

Our technical team is led by our senior Technical Specialist Mr. Badran, a co-founder. He is Jordanian, graduated from the German National Training Institute in Jordan in 1990 under supervision of GTZ, Royal Jordanian Medical Service, and Ministry of Health in Jordan. He provided consultancy and technical support to many Jordanian medical establishments. In 1994 he joined the International Committee of Red Cross (ICRC) in Geneva, Switzerland. He studied polypropylene prosthesis technology and worked internationally as an expert in Ethiopia, Pakistan, Afghanistan, and Turkmenistan. He joined Saudi German Hospital in 1999 and led the workshop department to international standards. He specializes in plastic jackets, Milwaukee corsets, shenue jackets, Boston corsets, baby TEV splints, and AFO splints. Atrafi is proud that Mr. Badran continues to lead its technical services since its establishment in 2017. Read More

Co-Founder & Director

Dr. YASSER EID MD. MBA.

Dr. Yasser Eid is an orthopedic surgeon with an MBA from Leicester University, UK. He has over 25 years of experience managing private healthcare facilities in GCC, specializing in orthopedics, physical medicine, orthotics, prosthetics, and rehabilitation. He has collaborated with international experts from Germany, UK, France, Austria, Switzerland, and USA. His involvement in technical, scientific, educational, and training activities has added value to Atrafi's overall performance. Dr. Yasser is an active member of the American Medical Director Association, Egyptian Medical Syndicate, Middle East Spine Group, American Society of Quality, and American Academy of Minimal Invasive Spine Medicine & Surgery.
